Frikadeller

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons onion, grated or chopped
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 dash of pepper

Steps

  1. Mix flour and lightly beaten egg and milk in small bowl
  2. Place pork in large bowl and mix with electric mixer (with dough hook)
  3. Add flour mix in small amounts until a soft, but not runny, mixture forms. (You may not need all of the mixture)
  4. Fold in pepper and onions into mixture
  5. Melt 1/4 cup butter in large pan
  6. Use a tablespoon to form frikadeller before placing them in pan
  7. Cook until brown, then turn. They should be well-browned.
  8. Check for doneness before removing from pan
