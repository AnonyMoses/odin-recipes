Banana Bread

Description

As the name states, it's a bread like cake made with banana as its prime ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups mashed bananas (about 3 bananas)
  • 1/3 cup low-fat plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt, stirring with a whisk
  3. Place sugar and butter in large bowl and beat with mixer for 1 minute
  4. Add eggs (one at a time)
  5. Add banana, yogurt, and vanilla, then mix
  6. Add flour mixture, beat until moist
  7. Spoon batter into 8x4 loaf pan coated with cooking spray
  8. Bake for 1 hour and check for doneness with toothpick
  9. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan
  10. Cool completely
